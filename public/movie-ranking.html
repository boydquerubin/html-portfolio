Boyd's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time

Interview with the Vampire

I love the character creation, pacing and story telling in this movie. It has great acting and amazing set design.

The Lord of the Rings (Trilogy)

These are probably the best made movies of all time.
It's a trilogy, I can't choose any of the three so I am lumping all of them in one.
They used real humans with prosthetics, mixed with CGI, which I thought made the movies that much better.
Classic movie about Good and Evil, friend and foe, action, romance, comedy, it has it all.

Jojo Rabbit

I had to watch this for one of my college humanities classes, and it made an impression on me at that specific point in my life.
The mixture of comedy set in a serious time period of World War II, worked for me.
I usually have a hard time watching movies set in the World Wars, but this movie was a really good watch.
